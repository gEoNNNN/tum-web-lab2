Our Café Journey

2015 – The Beginning: A Dream is Brewed

A small coffee shop opens in the heart of Chisinau, fueled by a passion for quality coffee and community. The journey begins with ethically sourced beans and handcrafted blends.

2017 – First Expansion: A Growing Community

With overwhelming support, a second location opens. The brand becomes known for its signature drinks, cozy ambiance, and commitment to excellence.

2019 – Specialty Coffee Recognition

Awarded for excellence in specialty coffee, the brand solidifies its reputation. Direct trade partnerships begin, ensuring fair wages for farmers and premium beans for customers.

2021 – Nationwide Growth

The brand expands across the country, introducing new locations, artisanal pastries, and seasonal blends inspired by global coffee traditions.

2023 – Sustainability Initiative

A commitment to sustainability takes center stage. Biodegradable packaging, reusable cup programs, and partnerships with eco-friendly farms define this new era.

2025 – Global Reach

The brand goes international, bringing its passion for specialty coffee to a global audience while staying true to its mission of quality, community, and sustainability.
